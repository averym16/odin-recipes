Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • softened salted butter
  • sugar
  • brown sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • all-purpose flour
  • baking soda
  • sea salt
  • chocolate chips
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder
  3. cream butter and sugar
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  5. Add chocolate Chips
  6. Shape cookies into balls and put them on sheet
  7. Bake for 10 minutes
