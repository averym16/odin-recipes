Taco Recipe

Ingredients

  • lean ground beef
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • salt
  • dried oregano
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • tomato sauce
  • water
  • favorite toppings

Steps

  1. add beef, chili powder, cumin, salt, oregano, garlic pepper to skillet and stir
  2. add tomato sauce and water
  3. Warm taco shells
  4. Fill taco shells with meat and favorite toppings
